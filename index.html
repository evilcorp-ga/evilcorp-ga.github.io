Registering your nick

the first thing you need to do after visiting for the first time is to register your nick. Registration of nick is pretty simple and strightforward. In case this is your first time on IRC server, the command is

/msg NickServ register mySecurePassword me@email.com

Needless to say change password and email values and select a very secure password.Once registered you will need your password to identify with your nick the next time you login.

Changing nicks

Now you want to change your nick to something new.Well the good news is its pretty simple to do that. Just use the following command for that

/nick new_nick

This will change your current nick to new_nick across all channels.

Logging in with registered nick

Once you have registered your nick, the next time you login , you need to identify yourself with password associated with the nick. That is simply as shown below.

/msg NickServ identify mySecurePassword

Private message

You can start private chat with any user using following command

/msg username message

Kick users

This option is available only for chat moderators.

/kick username
